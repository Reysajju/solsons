About Solson

We're on a mission to democratize publishing and help creators share their ideas with the world.

Our Story

Solson was founded with a simple belief: every creator deserves the opportunity to share their ideas with the world. We've seen too many brilliant minds struggle with the complexities of traditional publishing, watching their valuable content remain locked away from the audiences who need it most.

That's why we built the world's most comprehensive digital publishing platform. We understand the challenges creators face and have developed innovative solutions that make publishing accessible, profitable, and global.

Our Mission

To democratize publishing by providing creators worldwide with the tools, distribution, and support they need to transform their ideas into successful published content that reaches global audiences.

Why We're Different

  • Global Reach - Instant distribution to 150+ countries worldwide
  • Creator-First - Up to 70% royalties and full creative control
  • All Content Types - Books, articles, multimedia, courses, and more
  • Instant Publishing - Go live within 24 hours, not months
  • Advanced Analytics - Detailed insights into your audience and earnings
  • 24/7 Support - Dedicated team to help you succeed

Our Impact

Over the past 5 years, we've helped over 50,000 creators publish more than 2 million pieces of content. Our platform has facilitated over $50 million in creator earnings and reached readers in every corner of the globe.
